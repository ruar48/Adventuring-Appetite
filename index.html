We Provide the Best Quality
Services Ever

A food truck business is a great place to start for those looking to venture out into the restaurant industry. The operational costs are lower, you’ll need fewer staff, and you can follow the crowds instead of hoping they come to you. But along with these benefits come other operational challenges, like figuring out fuel and power, working out parking permits, and cooking and serving in a tiny space, but we know, we can do it. We are going to expand our business and we are open for Franchise We chose Food Truck as our Business Proposal, because we all know that everyone is a food lover. After getting our business permit, we can go where we want to sell. We are going to sell hamburger, potato corners, spaghetti, we can also add shawarma in our menu, and a drinks. I know we are going to love and enjoy our business because aside from selling, we are like having our adventure.

Discover the Perfect Pair:
Milk Tea & Fries

Our food truck brings you the ultimate comfort combo: creamy milk tea paired with crispy, golden fries.
